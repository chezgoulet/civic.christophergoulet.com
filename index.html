01 — MUNICIPAL SERVICE

Governing through a crisis.

Sworn in about two weeks before lockdowns began. Very hard choices at almost every meeting through the worst of the pandemic. I did my best to act in a manner that would let me sleep at night.

Permanent wins: a Pride flag permanently raised over town hall during Pride month. Retail cannabis passed in town limits. Taxes kept low during the worst economic upheaval in memory. Historic investments in infrastructure. Funds found for more playgrounds.

I spoke truth to power and moved the needle.

I learned how a municipality works — the layers and dynamics of power, market, and people and how they all interact.

Founder and Scoutmaster (Chief Tiguidou). Chartered under Outdoor Service Guides (OSG). All-adult, community defense-oriented, democratic governance.

"Find your path, change the world!"

Core Training

Skills That Matter

De-escalation and crisis communication. Wilderness First Responder. Emergency preparedness. HAM radio / emergency communications. Backcountry navigation. Community safety.

A note on framing: the 161st Catamounts are explicitly not a militia. We operate with democratic governance, mandatory background checks, JEDI principles, community accountability, and a de-escalation-first philosophy. The distinction matters and the distinguishing factors are obvious to anyone who engages with us.
