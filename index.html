02 — Community

Get together. Get good at skills. Go on adventures.

A community scouting troop I founded. That's the whole pitch.

I founded the 161st Catamounts and serve as Scoutmaster — Chief Tiguidou — under an Outdoor Service Guides charter. Three things: get together, get good at real skills, go on adventures.

The skills are the kind you'd actually want: de-escalation, Wilderness First Responder, HAM radio, backcountry travel. The point is young people who can take care of themselves and each other.

"Find your path, change the world!"

I was also a founding member of Whatdothlife, a Vermont music and arts collective that I helped navigate through 501(c)(3) status. Built the scene, the culture, the events, the shows. A community of artists, musicians, and builders making things happen. The unglamorous part — showing up early, staying late, making sure the magic has a place to land.

03 — Offerings

How I can help your organization work.

Cooperatives, municipalities, mutual aid groups, clubs, NGOs — if your mission isn't just profit, your operations should reflect that. Culture, policy, logistics, process. How decisions get made and power gets distributed.

Governance & Decision Design

How do you actually make decisions? Who has power, when, and how's it distributed? I help boards, co-ops, and member orgs design governance that matches their values — not corporate boilerplate.

Cooperative Operations & Process

The systems that keep values-based work running day to day. Logistics, workflows, meeting structures, operational rhythms. I bring what I learned converting a 30-year bakery to worker ownership — the process side of collective management.

Culture, Policy & Integration

Bringing cooperative culture into existing organizations, or building it from nothing. Policy that reflects real values, not boilerplate. Making distributed power work in practice, not just on paper.
